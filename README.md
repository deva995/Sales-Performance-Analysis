# Sales-Performance-Analysis
Sales Performance Analysis to Identify Revenue Leakage &amp; Growth Opportunities
## Problem
Sales revenue stagnation despite increased activity.

## Approach
Segmented data by region, product, and sales reps to identify performance drivers.

## Key Insights
- Revenue leakage driven by excessive discounting
- Underperforming regions with pricing inefficiencies
- Top reps outperform with lower discounts

## Recommendations
- Implement discount caps
- Replicate high-performer pricing strategies
- Shift focus from volume to revenue quality

## Tools Used
Excel, SQL, Power BI
